About Us

LETILICA Sdn. Bhd. is a Bumiputera company incorporated on 13 May 2019. We are a leading provider of advanced embedded electronics for automated systems in both industrial and military-grade applications.

Leveraging the latest technologies in advanced microprocessor and MCU hardware, we specialize in the development, manufacturing, and deployment of high-performance military drone platforms and standalone auxiliary guidance systems.

Our drones are engineered to meet demanding operational requirements, offering extended flight time, high reliability, and adaptability to the specific needs of end users.

Vision

To be the first Malaysian leader in military automated drone technology, transforming modern defence operations through innovation, precision, and reliability with primary objective of being fully self sustained in terms of design and manufacturing complete systems, both hardware and software without any external OEM.

Mission

Our mission is to provide state-of-the-art, mission-critical automated air, land and sea drone systems that enhance the operational capabilities of defence organizations while ensuring the safety of military personnel and the effectiveness of military strategies.
